Hi! This is a prototype web app designed to provide access and
insight into the many unapparent city services. It provides direct
access to St. Louis City's internal database in order look up activity
on your street in the last two weeks, or a search feature for all activity
in the last 8 years.
This has been created by Andrew Lutz in order to demonstrate both ability
and interest in the feild of software development in order to assist in
obtaining a position as a software developer.
I hope you find it both interesting and entertaining. To start, create an account
by clicking 'New User' and dive in !
